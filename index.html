Vanderbilt University  -  Chelsea Campus  -  New York City
A proposal for
The
Living
Gallery
Art, conversation, and music - woven into campus life.

Chelsea is the center of the American art world. This program turns that geography into a living asset - rotating exhibitions, artist-led discourse, and intimate performance, anchoring Vanderbilt as a cultural presence the neighborhood actually wants.

Three streams
I
Rotating Exhibition

Works from neighboring galleries cycling through campus every 6-8 weeks. A gallery that never stays still.

II
Culture Talks

Creatives, curators, critics - in real conversation. Cross-disciplinary, community-facing, intellectually alive.

III
Live Performance

Chamber, jazz, contemporary. Intimate and carefully chosen - a venue with genuine character.

What it delivers
Local Credibility

Vanderbilt as a member of Chelsea's cultural life, not a tenant of it.

Elevated Events

Every donor reception and alumni evening set against a living exhibition.

Community Reach

Programming that draws the arts world onto campus and builds lasting ties.

Zero Overhead

All curation, logistics, and production managed end-to-end by the proposer.
